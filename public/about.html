About


Welcome to WeatherWear! WeatherWear is designed to simplify your life. How do we do it? Let me explain.



WeatherWear takes forecast data for your location (from the Open Weather Map API) and provides you with what you need to know MOST about any forecast... WHAT. TO. WEAR! for that day.



Sign up for a FREE account and SET your location. WeatherWear tells you the outfit you need for that day based on the weather. Update your controls if you're someone who is "always hot," or "always cold," no matter the conditions. That's it. It's that simple. Happy Weathering!
